Services

Youth Villages provides help for children and young people across the United States who face a wide range of emotional, mental and behavioral problems. We work to find solutions using models that strengthen the child’s family and support systems and dramatically improve their long-term success. Youth Villages provides services ranging from in-home to residential treatment; shares its program models with partners; and supports state child welfare leaders to bring sustainable change to systems.

Youth Villages’ approach is to help our nation’s most vulnerable and troubled children. All our programs and services operate under an approach that emphasizes family, measurement, community, intensity and accountability.

Us Create Change

Every day, we serve thousands of children and their families, and we need your help. By partnering with public agencies and private providers, Youth Villages creates effective, research-based interventions for youth and families. We seek to improve the lives of others by training on specific service models, scaling best practices, pursuing innovation, informing policy and giving a voice to those we serve.
